The Funniest Pranks to Pull on Your Friends (Without Getting in Trouble)

Pranks can be a fun way to bond with your friends, but it's important to keep them harmless and respectful. If you're looking for some funny and safe prank ideas, you've come to the right place.

From classic pranks like putting a whoopee cushion on a chair to more creative pranks like switching out the sugar for salt, here are some of the funniest pranks to pull on your friends without getting in trouble.

Classic Pranks

Classic pranks are always a hit. They may be old-fashioned, but they never fail to get a laugh. Here are some of the best classic pranks to pull on your friends:

  • Whoopee cushion: Place a whoopee cushion on a chair and watch your friend jump when they sit down.
  • Fake spider: Place a fake spider on your friend's shoulder and watch them jump.
  • Fake vomit: Place a fake vomit on the floor and watch your friend's reaction when they see it.
  • Fake snake: Place a fake snake in a drawer or cupboard and watch your friend's reaction when they open it.

Creative Pranks

If you're looking for something a bit more creative, here are some of the funniest pranks to pull on your friends:

  • Switch the sugar for salt: Switch the sugar for salt in your friend's coffee or tea and watch their reaction when they take a sip.
  • Switch the milk for orange juice: Switch the milk for orange juice in your friend's cereal and watch their reaction when they take a bite.
  • Switch the shampoo for conditioner: Switch the shampoo for conditioner in your friend's shower and watch their reaction when they wash their hair.
  • Switch the remote for a toy: Switch the remote for a toy in your friend's living room and watch their reaction when they try to turn on the TV.

Prank Ideas for the Brave

If you're feeling brave, here are some of the funniest pranks to pull on your friends:

  • Hide a rubber snake in their bed: Hide a rubber snake in your friend's bed and watch their reaction when they get in.
  • Hide a fake spider in their car: Hide a fake spider in your friend's car and watch their reaction when they get in.
  • Hide a fake rat in their kitchen: Hide a fake rat in your friend's kitchen and watch their reaction when they open the cupboard.
  • Hide a fake cockroach in their bathroom: Hide a fake cockroach in your friend's bathroom and watch their reaction when they turn on the light.

Pranks can be a fun way to bond with your friends, but it's important to keep them harmless and respectful. With these funny and safe prank ideas, you can have a good laugh without getting in trouble.
