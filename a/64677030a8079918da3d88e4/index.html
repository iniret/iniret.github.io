Revolutionize Your Tea Time: Unusual Fillings for Tea Sandwiches

Tea sandwiches are a classic staple of afternoon tea. They are small, bite-sized sandwiches that are usually served on white bread with the crusts cut off. While traditional fillings such as cucumber and cream cheese, egg salad, and smoked salmon are delicious, why not step out of your comfort zone and try something extraordinary? Here are some unusual fillings to make your tea sandwiches stand out.

Hummus and Roasted Red Pepper

Hummus is a delicious and nutritious spread made from chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, and lemon juice. It is a great source of protein and fiber, and it is also vegan and gluten-free. When paired with roasted red peppers, it makes a flavorful and colorful filling for tea sandwiches.

Avocado and Bacon

Avocado and bacon is a classic combination that is sure to please. The creamy texture of the avocado is a perfect contrast to the crunchy bacon. This filling is sure to be a hit with both vegetarians and meat-eaters alike.

Cream Cheese and Pickles

Cream cheese and pickles may sound like an odd combination, but it is surprisingly delicious. The tangy pickles are a great contrast to the creamy cheese, and the combination is sure to be a hit with your guests.

Peanut Butter and Jelly

Peanut butter and jelly is a classic combination that never goes out of style. It is a great way to add a bit of nostalgia to your tea sandwiches. For a more grown-up version, try using a gourmet jelly or jam.

Brie and Apple

Brie and apple is a classic combination that is sure to please. The creamy brie pairs perfectly with the sweet and tart apple. This filling is sure to be a hit with your guests.

Ricotta and Spinach

Ricotta and spinach is a great combination for tea sandwiches. The ricotta adds a creamy texture, while the spinach adds a bit of crunch. This filling is sure to be a hit with your guests.

Tuna and Cucumber

Tuna and cucumber is a classic combination that is sure to please. The tuna adds a bit of protein, while the cucumber adds a bit of crunch. This filling is sure to be a hit with your guests.

Tea sandwiches are a classic staple of afternoon tea, and they are a great way to add a bit of variety to your tea time. With these unusual fillings, you can revolutionize your tea time and make your tea sandwiches stand out. So, why not step out of your comfort zone and try something extraordinary?
