10 Timeless Lipstick Shades Every Woman Should Own

When it comes to makeup, there are few products as iconic as lipstick. From classic reds to modern nudes, there are countless shades to choose from. But which ones should you have in your makeup bag? Here are 10 timeless lipstick shades that every woman should own.

Red

No makeup collection is complete without a classic red lipstick. Whether you prefer a bright cherry or a deep burgundy, a red lipstick is a must-have. Red lipstick is perfect for a night out or a special occasion. It's also a great way to add a pop of color to any look.

Nude

Nude lipsticks are a great way to add a subtle hint of color to your look. From light pinks to browns, there are countless shades to choose from. Nude lipsticks are perfect for everyday wear and can be paired with any look.

Pink

Pink lipsticks are a great way to add a feminine touch to your look. From soft baby pinks to bright fuchsias, there are countless shades to choose from. Pink lipsticks are perfect for a romantic date night or a day out with friends.

Orange

Orange lipsticks are a great way to add a bold pop of color to your look. From bright tangerines to deep corals, there are countless shades to choose from. Orange lipsticks are perfect for a night out or a special occasion.

Plum

Plum lipsticks are a great way to add a hint of drama to your look. From soft mauves to deep purples, there are countless shades to choose from. Plum lipsticks are perfect for a night out or a special occasion.

Brown

Brown lipsticks are a great way to add a subtle hint of color to your look. From light taupes to deep chocolates, there are countless shades to choose from. Brown lipsticks are perfect for everyday wear and can be paired with any look.

Burgundy

Burgundy lipsticks are a great way to add a hint of drama to your look. From deep reds to dark purples, there are countless shades to choose from. Burgundy lipsticks are perfect for a night out or a special occasion.

Peach

Peach lipsticks are a great way to add a subtle hint of color to your look. From light corals to bright peaches, there are countless shades to choose from. Peach lipsticks are perfect for everyday wear and can be paired with any look.

Coral

Coral lipsticks are a great way to add a bold pop of color to your look. From bright oranges to deep pinks, there are countless shades to choose from. Coral lipsticks are perfect for a night out or a special occasion.

Berry

Berry lipsticks are a great way to add a hint of drama to your look. From soft plums to deep berries, there are countless shades to choose from. Berry lipsticks are perfect for a night out or a special occasion.

No matter what your style is, there's a timeless lipstick shade for everyone. From classic reds to modern nudes, these 10 shades are must-haves for any makeup collection. So go ahead and find your perfect match!
