Makeup Routines of Iconic Hollywood Stars: Secrets Revealed

We all know that Hollywood stars have access to the best makeup artists and products, but what are their secrets to achieving that perfect look? Get an inside look at the beauty routines of some of the most iconic Hollywood stars, and learn how you can achieve their glamorous looks.

Marilyn Monroe

Marilyn Monroe was known for her classic beauty and signature red lips. Her makeup artist, Allan "Whitey" Snyder, revealed that he used Max Factor Pan-Cake Makeup to create her iconic look. He would apply the foundation with a damp sponge and then use a brush to blend it in. He would then use a black eyeliner to create a cat-eye shape and finish off with a few coats of mascara. To get her signature red lips, he used Max Factor's Fire and Ice lipstick.

Audrey Hepburn

Audrey Hepburn was known for her timeless beauty and natural makeup look. Her makeup artist, Alberto de Rossi, revealed that he used Chanel's Vitalumiere foundation to create a flawless base. He would then use a brown eyeliner to define her eyes and finish off with a few coats of mascara. To get her signature look, he used Chanel's Rouge Coco lipstick.

Elizabeth Taylor

Elizabeth Taylor was known for her glamorous and bold makeup looks. Her makeup artist, Kevyn Aucoin, revealed that he used Dior's Diorskin foundation to create a flawless base. He would then use a black eyeliner to create a dramatic cat-eye shape and finish off with a few coats of mascara. To get her signature look, he used Dior's Rouge Dior lipstick.

Angelina Jolie

Angelina Jolie is known for her sultry and smoky makeup looks. Her makeup artist, Toni G, revealed that he used Lancome's Teint Idole foundation to create a flawless base. He would then use a black eyeliner to create a smoky eye and finish off with a few coats of mascara. To get her signature look, he used Lancome's L'Absolu Rouge lipstick.

Conclusion

These iconic Hollywood stars have all achieved their signature looks with the help of their makeup artists and the right products. While it may seem intimidating to recreate their looks, it is possible with the right products and techniques. So, if you're looking to achieve a glamorous Hollywood look, try out some of these products and techniques and you'll be sure to turn heads!
