The Best Chick Flicks to Watch With Your Girlfriends

Are you looking for a movie night with your girlfriends? Whether you're looking for a romantic comedy, a tear-jerker, or a classic, this post has you covered. Here are the best chick flicks that will have you laughing, crying, and swooning. So grab some popcorn and get ready for a night of girl power!

Romantic Comedies

Romantic comedies are always a great choice for a movie night with your girlfriends. Here are some of the best romantic comedies to watch with your friends:

  • The Proposal: This classic romantic comedy stars Sandra Bullock and Ryan Reynolds as a mismatched couple who must pretend to be engaged in order to save Bullock's character from deportation. It's a hilarious and heartwarming story that will have you laughing and crying.

  • Crazy Rich Asians: This modern romantic comedy follows a young woman who discovers that her boyfriend is from a wealthy and powerful family in Singapore. It's a funny and romantic story that will have you rooting for the couple.

  • 10 Things I Hate About You: This classic teen romantic comedy stars Heath Ledger and Julia Stiles as two high schoolers who must overcome their differences in order to be together. It's a funny and heartwarming story that will have you laughing and swooning.

Tear-Jerkers

Sometimes you just need a good cry. Here are some of the best tear-jerkers to watch with your girlfriends:

  • The Notebook: This classic romantic drama stars Ryan Gosling and Rachel McAdams as a couple who must overcome obstacles in order to be together. It's a heartbreaking and romantic story that will have you in tears.

  • Love, Simon: This modern romantic drama follows a young man who must come to terms with his sexuality and find the courage to come out to his family and friends. It's a heartfelt and inspiring story that will have you in tears.

  • The Fault in Our Stars: This heartbreaking romantic drama stars Shailene Woodley and Ansel Elgort as two teenagers who must come to terms with their terminal illnesses. It's a heartbreaking and inspiring story that will have you in tears.

Classics

Sometimes you just need a classic. Here are some of the best classic chick flicks to watch with your girlfriends:

  • Dirty Dancing: This classic romantic drama stars Patrick Swayze and Jennifer Grey as two unlikely lovers who must overcome obstacles in order to be together. It's a funny and romantic story that will have you laughing and swooning.

  • Pretty Woman: This classic romantic comedy stars Richard Gere and Julia Roberts as a mismatched couple who must overcome their differences in order to be together. It's a funny and romantic story that will have you laughing and swooning.

  • Sixteen Candles: This classic teen romantic comedy stars Molly Ringwald as a young woman who must overcome her insecurities in order to find true love. It's a funny and heartwarming story that will have you laughing and swooning.

So grab some popcorn and get ready for a night of girl power with these best chick flicks. Whether you're looking for a romantic comedy, a tear-jerker, or a classic, this post has you covered.
