The Best Twilight Fan Theories That Will Blow Your Mind

Twilight has a huge fanbase, and with that comes some pretty wild fan theories. From the possibility of vampires existing in the real world to the idea that Bella Swan is actually a vampire, these theories will leave you questioning everything. Here are some of the best fan theories about the Twilight series that will blow your mind.

Bella Swan is a Vampire

One of the most popular fan theories about the Twilight series is that Bella Swan is actually a vampire. This theory suggests that Bella was born a vampire, but her parents kept it a secret from her. This would explain why she was so drawn to Edward and why she was so easily able to accept his vampire nature. It would also explain why she was so resistant to the idea of becoming a vampire herself.

Edward is a Time Traveler

Another popular fan theory is that Edward is actually a time traveler. This theory suggests that Edward is from the future and has traveled back in time to meet Bella. This would explain why he is so much older than her and why he is so knowledgeable about the future. It would also explain why he is so protective of her and why he is so determined to keep her safe.

The Volturi are Aliens

This fan theory suggests that the Volturi, the powerful vampire coven that rules over the vampires in the Twilight series, are actually aliens. This theory suggests that the Volturi are from another planet and have come to Earth to take over the vampire population. This would explain why they are so powerful and why they are so feared by the other vampires.

The Cullens are Angels

This fan theory suggests that the Cullens, the vampire family that Bella joins in the Twilight series, are actually angels. This theory suggests that the Cullens are sent from Heaven to protect Bella and keep her safe from the Volturi. This would explain why they are so powerful and why they are so determined to protect Bella.

Vampires Exist in the Real World

This fan theory suggests that vampires actually exist in the real world. This theory suggests that vampires have been living among us for centuries and that they are just as powerful and dangerous as they are in the Twilight series. This would explain why vampires have been so difficult to find and why they have been able to remain hidden for so long.

These are just a few of the many fan theories about the Twilight series that have been circulating for years. Whether you believe them or not, they are sure to leave you questioning everything you thought you knew about the series. So, the next time you watch the Twilight movies, keep an open mind and see if any of these theories make sense to you.
