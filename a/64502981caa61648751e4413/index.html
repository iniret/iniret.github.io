The Best Dad Jokes to Make You Groan and Laugh

Love them or hate them, dad jokes are a timeless form of comedy. Whether you’re a fan of the classic puns or the more modern quips, dad jokes are sure to make you groan and laugh. Here are some of the best dad jokes to make you chuckle.

Classic Dad Jokes

Dad jokes are known for their puns and wordplay. Here are some of the classic dad jokes that have been around for years:

  • Q: What did the fish say when it hit the wall?

  • A: Dam!

  • Q: What did the cheese say when it looked in the mirror?

  • A: Halloumi!

  • Q: What did the 0 say to the 8?

  • A: Nice belt!

Modern Dad Jokes

Modern dad jokes are often more relatable and topical. Here are some of the best modern dad jokes:

  • Q: What did the iPhone say to the Samsung?

  • A: I’ve got iOS and you don’t!

  • Q: What did the avocado say to the toast?

  • A: Avo-cuddle me!

  • Q: What did the traffic light say to the car?

  • A: Don’t look, I’m changing!

Dad Jokes for Kids

Dad jokes are often tailored to the age of the audience. Here are some of the best dad jokes for kids:

  • Q: What did the big flower say to the little flower?

  • A: Hi, bud!

  • Q: What did the bee say to the flower?

  • A: I'm just buzzing by to say hello!

  • Q: What did the tree say to the wind?

  • A: Leaf me alone!

No matter what type of dad jokes you prefer, there’s something for everyone. Whether you’re a fan of the classic puns or the more modern quips, dad jokes are sure to make you groan and laugh. So, the next time you’re looking for a good laugh, try out one of these dad jokes.
