10 Tinder Openers That Actually Work: Start the Conversation with Confidence

Tinder is a great way to meet new people and start conversations. But it can be hard to know how to break the ice and make a memorable first impression. That's why we've put together this list of 10 proven Tinder openers that will help you start the conversation with confidence.

1. Ask an Open-Ended Question

One of the best ways to start a conversation on Tinder is to ask an open-ended question. This will give the other person an opportunity to share something about themselves and start a conversation. Try asking something like, "What's the best thing that happened to you this week?" or "What's the most interesting thing you've done lately?"

2. Compliment Their Profile

Another great way to start a conversation on Tinder is to give the other person a compliment. This will show that you took the time to look at their profile and that you're interested in getting to know them. Try something like, "I love your profile picture! Where was it taken?" or "I love your taste in music. What's your favorite song?"

3. Share Something Interesting

Sharing something interesting about yourself is a great way to start a conversation on Tinder. This will give the other person an opportunity to ask questions and get to know you better. Try something like, "I just got back from a trip to Thailand. What's the most interesting place you've ever been?" or "I just started learning to play the guitar. What's the most interesting hobby you have?"

4. Ask About Their Interests

Asking about the other person's interests is a great way to start a conversation on Tinder. This will show that you're interested in getting to know them better and that you're open to talking about different topics. Try something like, "What do you like to do in your free time?" or "What's your favorite hobby?"

5. Talk About Your Shared Interests

If you have something in common with the other person, it's a great way to start a conversation on Tinder. This will show that you have something in common and that you're interested in getting to know them better. Try something like, "I see you like hiking. What's your favorite trail?" or "I see you're a fan of the same sports team. Who's your favorite player?"

6. Ask About Their Day

Asking about the other person's day is a great way to start a conversation on Tinder. This will show that you're interested in getting to know them better and that you care about how their day is going. Try something like, "How was your day?" or "What did you do today?"

7. Talk About Your Day

Talking about your day is another great way to start a conversation on Tinder. This will give the other person an opportunity to ask questions and get to know you better. Try something like, "I had a great day today. What did you do?" or "I had a really busy day. What did you do to relax?"

8. Ask for Advice

Asking for advice is a great way to start a conversation on Tinder. This will show that you're interested in getting to know the other person better and that you value their opinion. Try something like, "I'm looking for a new restaurant to try. Do you have any recommendations?" or "I'm looking for a new book to read. Do you have any suggestions?"

9. Talk About Your Goals

Talking about your goals is a great way to start a conversation on Tinder. This will give the other person an opportunity to ask questions and get to know you better. Try something like, "I'm working on a project to help the environment. What are you working on?" or "I'm trying to learn a new language. What are your goals?"

10. Share a Joke

Sharing a joke is a great way to start a conversation on Tinder. This will show that you have a sense of humor and that you're open to having a lighthearted conversation. Try something like, "What did the fish say when it hit the wall? Dam!" or "Why did the chicken cross the playground? To get to the other slide!"
