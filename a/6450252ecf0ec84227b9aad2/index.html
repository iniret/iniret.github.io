7 Inspiring Memoirs by Women You Need to Read Now

In recent years, there has been a surge of memoirs written by women that have captivated readers around the world. These powerful stories of incredible women and their journeys through life are must-reads for anyone looking for inspiration and motivation. Here are seven inspiring memoirs by women that you need to read now.

1. Becoming by Michelle Obama

The former First Lady of the United States, Michelle Obama, has written a powerful memoir about her life and her journey to becoming the woman she is today. In Becoming, Obama shares her story of growing up on the South Side of Chicago, her struggles with racism and sexism, and her journey to the White House. This inspiring memoir is a must-read for anyone looking for motivation and inspiration.

2. Educated by Tara Westover

Tara Westover's memoir, Educated, is a powerful story of her journey from growing up in a survivalist family in rural Idaho to becoming a PhD student at Cambridge University. Westover's story is an inspiring tale of resilience and determination, and her memoir is a must-read for anyone looking for motivation and inspiration.

3. The Glass Castle by Jeannette Walls

Jeannette Walls' memoir, The Glass Castle, is a powerful story of her childhood growing up in a dysfunctional family. Walls' story is an inspiring tale of resilience and determination, and her memoir is a must-read for anyone looking for motivation and inspiration.

4. Wild by Cheryl Strayed

Cheryl Strayed's memoir, Wild, is a powerful story of her journey of self-discovery as she hikes the Pacific Crest Trail. Strayed's story is an inspiring tale of resilience and determination, and her memoir is a must-read for anyone looking for motivation and inspiration.

5. I Know Why the Caged Bird Sings by Maya Angelou

Maya Angelou's memoir, I Know Why the Caged Bird Sings, is a powerful story of her childhood growing up in the segregated South. Angelou's story is an inspiring tale of resilience and determination, and her memoir is a must-read for anyone looking for motivation and inspiration.

6. The Year of Yes by Shonda Rhimes

Shonda Rhimes' memoir, The Year of Yes, is a powerful story of her journey of self-discovery as she embarks on a year-long journey of saying "yes" to everything. Rhimes' story is an inspiring tale of resilience and determination, and her memoir is a must-read for anyone looking for motivation and inspiration.

7. The Princess Diarist by Carrie Fisher

Carrie Fisher's memoir, The Princess Diarist, is a powerful story of her journey of self-discovery as she reflects on her time as Princess Leia in the original Star Wars trilogy. Fisher's story is an inspiring tale of resilience and determination, and her memoir is a must-read for anyone looking for motivation and inspiration.

These seven inspiring memoirs by women are must-reads for anyone looking for motivation and inspiration. From Michelle Obama's story of growing up on the South Side of Chicago to Carrie Fisher's reflections on her time as Princess Leia, these powerful stories of incredible women and their journeys through life are sure to inspire and motivate readers.
