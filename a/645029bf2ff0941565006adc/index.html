The Art of Layering: How to Layer Your Clothes Like a Streetstyle Pro

Layering can take your outfit from basic to bold. Mastering the art of layering is essential for creating a streetstyle look that will turn heads. Whether you’re looking to add a bit of extra warmth to your outfit or just want to add a bit of extra style, layering is the way to go.

Here are some tips on how to layer your clothes like a streetstyle pro:

Start with the Basics

The key to layering is to start with the basics. Choose a base layer such as a t-shirt or tank top and then build from there. This will help you create a cohesive look that won’t be too overwhelming.

Choose the Right Fabrics

When layering, it’s important to choose the right fabrics. Lightweight fabrics such as cotton and linen are great for layering as they won’t add too much bulk. Heavier fabrics such as wool and denim can also be used, but should be used sparingly.

Mix and Match

When layering, it’s important to mix and match different pieces. Try pairing a patterned shirt with a solid-colored jacket or a long cardigan with a short skirt. This will help create a unique look that will stand out from the crowd.

Accessorize

Accessories are a great way to add a bit of extra style to your layered look. Try adding a statement necklace or a bold belt to your outfit. This will help tie the look together and add a bit of extra flair.

Play with Proportions

When layering, it’s important to play with proportions. Try pairing a long cardigan with a short skirt or a cropped jacket with a long dress. This will help create a unique look that will be sure to turn heads.

Layering can be a great way to add a bit of extra style to your outfit. With the right pieces and a bit of creativity, you can create a streetstyle look that will be sure to turn heads. So go ahead and give it a try!
